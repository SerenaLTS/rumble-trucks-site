SITRAK G7S 8x4 Tipper Cab & Chassis

Maximum Payload Performance for Heavy Construction Operations.

The SITRAK G7S 8x4 Tipper Cab & Chassis is engineered for high-capacity construction, quarry and large-scale infrastructure transport across Australia. Featuring a powerful 12.42L engine, ZF TraXon automated transmission and reinforced 8x4 chassis configuration, the G7S 8x4 Tipper delivers exceptional torque output, balanced load distribution and long-term durability under demanding operating conditions.

Built to support heavy-duty tipper body integration, this configuration provides the structural strength, traction and stability required for modern civil and infrastructure projects.

Overview

Designed for heavy-duty tipping cycles and high-capacity payload operations, the G7S 8x4 Tipper combines strong engine performance, reinforced chassis engineering and advanced safety systems. Its 8x4 configuration ensures improved load distribution and stability, making it well-suited for demanding construction and quarry environments.

Applications

  • Quarry & Aggregate Haulage — Designed for sand, gravel, rock and bulk material transport.
  • Large-Scale Civil & Infrastructure Projects — Suitable for high-capacity construction supply and earthmoving support.
  • Heavy Construction Fleets — Built for high-frequency tipping cycles and demanding operational environments.
  • Custom Tipper Body Installations — Optimised for commercial body-builder compatibility.

Key Highlights

High-Output 12.42L Diesel Engine

  • Engine Model: 540HP MC13.54-61
  • Displacement: 12.42L
  • Max Power: 400 kW @ 1800 rpm
  • Max Torque: 2500 Nm @ 1000–1400 rpm

Provides strong low-end torque for loaded start-up and steep grade performance.

ZF TraXon 12-Speed AMT

  • Model: ZF 12TX2621TD
  • Smooth automated shifting
  • Matched PTO compatibility

Delivers efficient power transfer and reduced driver fatigue.

Heavy-Duty 8x4 Driveline

  • GVM: 31,000 kg
  • Front Axle Design Load: 11,500 kg (total)
  • Rear Axle Design Load: 18,000 kg (total)
  • Differential Locks: Interwheel & Interaxle
  • Main Drive Ratio: 3.08

Ensures stable traction and balanced load support on uneven surfaces.

Reinforced Tipper-Ready Frame

  • Main Beam Cross Section: 300 × 80 × 8 mm
  • Trapezoidal ladder-type chassis
  • Frame Width: Front 940 mm / Rear 850 mm

Engineered for durability under heavy-load tipping cycles.

Suspension & Stability

  • Front: Parabolic Leaf Springs (3/3)
  • Rear: 8 Airbag Air Suspension

Enhances load balance and ride control during site operations.

Advanced Braking & Safety

  • Dual Circuit Compressed Air Brake
  • EBS + ESC + HSA
  • ACC + AEBS + LDW
  • Spring Energy Air-Cut Parking Brake

Supports fleet safety compliance and operational reliability.
