Overview

The SITRAK C9H 6x4 Prime Mover is purpose-built for high-demand interstate transport and heavy combination applications across Australia. Combining a high-output 15.3L WP15H660E62A engine with a proven ZF TraXon 12-speed automated transmission, the C9H delivers exceptional torque, fuel efficiency and operational reliability.

Designed for B-Double and high-GCM applications, the C9H features a reinforced chassis, air suspension for driver comfort, and advanced safety systems — ideal for fleet operators and owner-drivers seeking long-term performance and reduced total cost of ownership.

Applications

  • Interstate Linehaul Transport — Long-distance freight operations across NSW, VIC, QLD and beyond.
  • B-Double & Heavy Combination Operations — Suitable for high GCM requirements up to 90,000 kg.
  • Fleet Logistics & Distribution — Optimised for high utilisation fleets with proven driveline components.
  • Container, Refrigerated & General Freight — Compatible with curtain-siders, skeletal, refrigerated and bulk freight trailers.

Key Highlights

Powerful 15.3L Engine Performance

  • Engine: WP15H660E62A
  • Displacement: 15.3L
  • Max Power: 485 kW @ 1800 rpm
  • Max Torque: 3200 Nm @ 1200–1400 rpm

Strong low-end torque ensures smooth take-off and confident climbing under heavy loads.

ZF TraXon Automated Transmission

  • Model: ZF 12TX3221TO (12-speed AMT)

Delivers smooth shifting, improved fuel economy and reduced driver fatigue.

Heavy-Duty Driveline & Axles

  • Front Axle: 7,000 kg
  • Rear Drive Axle: 18,000 kg
  • Differential Locks: Inter-axle & Cross-lock
  • Final Drive Ratio: 3.08 / 3.36 / 3.70

Built for durability in demanding Australian road conditions.

High GCM Capability

  • Kerb Weight: 9,400 kg
  • GVM: 25,000 kg
  • GCM: 90,000 kg
  • Max Gradeability: 23%

Designed for heavy combination and high-performance freight tasks.

Air Suspension Comfort & Stability

  • Front: Parabolic leaf springs
  • Rear: 8-airbag suspension system

Enhances ride quality and protects cargo integrity.

Advanced Safety Systems

  • Dual-circuit air brake system
  • EBS (Electronic Braking System)
  • ESC (Electronic Stability Control)
  • HSA (Hill Start Assist)
  • ACC + AEBS + LDW (as specified)

Supporting safer fleet operations and compliance requirements.

Long-Range Capability

  • Fuel Tank Capacity: 1,000 L
  • Electrical System: 24V
  • Alternator: 3 kW
  • Batteries: 2 × 12V / 240Ah

Minimises refuelling stops during long-haul operations.
