Heavy-Duty Power for Australian Linehaul Operations.

Overview

The SITRAK C9H 6x4 Prime Mover is engineered for Australia’s toughest long-haul tasks, combining a 15.3L high-output engine with a ZF TraXon 12-speed AMT to deliver strong pulling power, stable highway performance, and high uptime for linehaul fleets. Built for B-Double and heavy combination operations, it supports demanding duty cycles while helping reduce driver fatigue and overall operating costs.

What Makes It Different

The C9H combines European transmission technology with robust heavy-duty engineering to suit Australian interstate corridors. Its 3200 Nm torque band (1200–1400 rpm) supports confident take-off and climbing under heavy loads, while the reinforced chassis, air suspension stability and advanced safety suite help fleets maximise uptime and maintain compliance.
