OUR TRUCK RANGE

Explore the SITRAK range available through RUMBLE AUTO.
SITRAK offers a comprehensive heavy-duty truck lineup tailored to meet the diverse demands of the Australian transport and construction industries. Our range is structured into three core series — G5S, G7S and C9H — covering long-haul transport, construction, mining, logistics and municipal applications. From high-performance prime movers to versatile cab & chassis configurations, SITRAK trucks are engineered for reliability, efficiency and maximum uptime across every terrain.
